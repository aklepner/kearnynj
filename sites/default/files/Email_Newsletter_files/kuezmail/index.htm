Message from the Coordinator

Welcome to our second – and new and improved -- issue of the Kearny Urban Enterprise Zone (KUEZ) Members Newsletter.  Keeping you, our KUEZ members and the Kearny business community, informed and involved is the key to our mutual success.
Since starting as your KUEZ Coordinator in May 2009 we’ve kept very busy here at the UEZ office; and I’m proud of what we have achieved. This newsletter will be one way we will strive to keep you up to date on our accomplishments, available programs, and many new and exciting activities.
In the last months we’ve invested a lot of time letting people know about our UEZ, attracting more shoppers into Kearny, and energizing the Zone.  Have you seen our billboards?  What about our cable TV commercials? Last spring we launched our first-ever Internet ad campaign and expanded our newspaper advertising into new markets, co-oping ad buys with many of our member businesses. We’re once again hosting the Holiday Tree Lighting Festival, which has over the last few years emerged as one our most popular town-wide traditions. Over the last months we’ve also sponsored the annual Easter Egg Hunt, a Spring Mothers’ Day Sweepstakes, the Farmers Market (which continues through October 28th) and our newest marketing campaign, the Kearny UEZ Town-Wide Yard and Sidewalk Sale which takes place this weekend and will introduce our Zone to new shoppers.
Important KUEZ infrastructure projects also continue, such as the Kearny Avenue Streetscape, the Sellers Street Area Roadway Reconstruction and Drainage project, the Jacobus Avenue Reconstruction project, and the repaving of several important business arteries.
We are always looking to expand our membership so more Kearny businesses can take advantage of the benefits the KUEZ has to offer -- such as the 3 ½% sales tax, low-interest Micro Loan and Revolving Loan Programs, and the Tax Exempt Status that our members enjoy, to name a few.  So we welcome your help in spreading the word about the Zone!
As always, we are very receptive to the business needs in the community and we’ll do whatever we can to help fulfill those needs. You can always reach me at 201-955-7400 (ext.8041) or jpeneda@kearnynj.org. Looking forward to hearing from you soon!

New Retail Promotion Set for October 16
KEARNY TOWN-WIDE YARD and SIDEWALK SALE

Kearny residents are busy cleaning out their closets, attics and garages and getting ready for the first annual "Town Wide Yard and Sidewalk Sale" on Saturday, October 16 from 9 to 4 p.m. Rain date is Saturday, October 23. "We're hoping this event helps energize the town and brings new visitors and shoppers into the Zone," said UEZ department head John Peneda.
KUEZ businesses are encouraged to capitalize on what is expected to be an influx of shoppers to the town that day and sponsor special sales.
Here's how it works: Any resident in town can have a yard sale that day at their home; and KUEZ members are being contacted and asked to join in the sale.
KUEZ members hosting sales, as well as residential participants, will be included in a special Yard and Sidewalk Sale Map that will be distributed throughout Town and available on line for visiting shoppers. This way, buyers know where to stop and shop!
The Yard and Sidewalk sale will be aggressively advertised not only here in town, but also to several neighboring communities. "Many other towns and communities have had great success with promotions such as these," said Coordinator Peneda. "We're hoping for similar success here in Kearny."
For more information call the KUEZ office. Click on the map to download a copy.

HALLOWEEN FLOAT
KUEZ Families Invited to Join the Parade

No Tricks! Just a special treat for UEZ members and their families this Halloween.
Each year, the KUEZ sponsors a float in the Kearny Annual Halloween Parade. "This year, we are inviting our members and their families to join us in the festivities," said Coordinator Peneda. Children ages 7 to 15 years old are welcome to ride the float; however, children up to age 12 must be accompanied by an adult. Children and adults must be in costume. Space is limited, so contact the KUEZ office at 201-955-7400 x 8041 or email John Peneda at jpeneda@kearnynj.org as soon as possible
to save a space!
This year's Parade will be held on Halloween, Sunday, October 31.

NEW FIRE TRUCK for KFD

Thanks in part to the UEZ, the people of Kearny will now benefit from two new fire trucks purchased for our Kearny Fire Department (KFD).
The first, a new custom pumper fire engine, was delivered to the KFD in June of last year. Of the total $419,318 cost of the vehicle, $210,000 was underwritten by the KUEZ; the town matched the difference.
A second new 2009 custom pumper is on now schedule to be delivered to the fire department early next year.   ...read more
